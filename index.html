Our Story

Where Health Meets Bold Flavor

Nestled in the Westerleigh neighborhood of Staten Island, Red Sea Restaurant & Detox Juice Bar was born from two passions: nourishing the body with pure, cold-pressed juices and satisfying the soul with expertly grilled kababs and authentic Mediterranean cuisine.

Everything is made fresh to order. No shortcuts, no compromises. Our team greets every customer with warmth because we're not just a restaurant — we're a community fixture in Staten Island.
