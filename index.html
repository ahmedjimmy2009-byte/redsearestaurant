Our Story

Born From the Sea,
Made With Heart

"We cook the way our families taught us — with fresh ingredients, unhurried technique, and enough love that you can taste it in every dish."

Red Sea Restaurant began with a simple belief: that real, fresh food — cold-pressed juices, nourishing smoothies, and flame-grilled kebabs — prepared with care and made to order, can bring people together in ways that few things can.

Nestled in the heart of Staten Island, we've built something that feels less like a restaurant and more like a gathering place. Families celebrate here. Friends catch up here. Couples come back, year after year — because the food is honest, the welcome is warm, and the experience is always worth it.

Every recipe carries a story — of Mediterranean coastlines, of family kitchens, of long dinners and longer conversations. We bring that spirit to every plate, whether you're dining with us or ordering from home.
